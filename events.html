🌙 Sindhi Festival

Teejri — Festival of Devotion

Teejri is a beautiful Sindhi festival observed on the third day after the full moon of the Shravan month (July/August). Celebrated primarily by married women for the well-being and longevity of their husbands, this festival is rich in tradition — from the pre-dawn Asur meal of Lola and Koki, to the evening Teejri Katha, and the sacred Arghya offering of rice, milk, and cucumber to the moon at moonrise. Women adorn themselves in traditional attire, apply mehndi, and gather to sing and celebrate together.
