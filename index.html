Our Mission

Dedicated to nurturing the Sindhi identity and fostering a vibrant community in the Pacific Northwest

Cultural Preservation

Keeping the rich heritage of Sindhi traditions, language, and customs alive for future generations through community gatherings and cultural programs.

Community Building

Creating a warm and welcoming space for Sindhi families in Seattle to connect, celebrate, and support each other through life's journey.

Spiritual Growth

Organizing religious ceremonies, satsangs, and celebrations of festivals like Cheti Chand, Diwali, and Teejri to nourish our spiritual roots.

Est. Seattle, WA

Preserving Our Heritage in the Pacific Northwest

The Sindhi Association of Seattle is a non-profit organization committed to bringing the Sindhi community together in the greater Seattle area. We celebrate the richness of our Sindhi heritage through cultural events, religious celebrations, and community gatherings.

Our association serves as a bridge between generations, ensuring that the values, language, and traditions of the Sindhi people continue to thrive far from our ancestral homeland.
