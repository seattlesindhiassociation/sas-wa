# 🙏 Sindhi Association of Seattle (SAS-WA)

**Bringing Sindhi Community Together**

The official website for the Sindhi Association of Seattle — a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to promoting and preserving Hindu Sindhi culture, language, and values in the greater Seattle area.

🌐 **Live Site:** [seattlesindhiassociation.github.io/sas-wa](https://seattlesindhiassociation.github.io/sas-wa/)

---

## About

The Sindhi Association of Seattle serves as a home for the Sindhi community in the Pacific Northwest. We organize cultural events, religious celebrations, and community gatherings that keep our heritage alive for future generations.

### What We Do
- 🪔 **Cultural Celebrations** — Cheti Chand, Diwali, Teejri, Lal Loi
- 📖 **Language Preservation** — Sindhi language programs and storytelling
- 🤝 **Community Building** — Picnics, potlucks, and family gatherings
- 🙏 **Spiritual Programs** — Satsangs, bhajans, and prayer ceremonies
- 👦 **Youth Programs** — Cultural workshops and mentoring for young Sindhis
