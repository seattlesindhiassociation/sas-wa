Our Story

The Sindhi Association of Seattle was founded with a simple yet profound vision — to create a home away from home for the Sindhi community in the Pacific Northwest. What started as small gatherings of a few Sindhi families has grown into a vibrant community organization.

Sindhis, originally from the Sindh region, have a rich and diverse cultural heritage that spans thousands of years. After the partition of 1947, millions of Sindhis were displaced from their homeland, yet they carried their culture, traditions, and indomitable spirit wherever they went.

Today, the Sindhi diaspora in Seattle continues this legacy, and our association serves as the custodian of these precious traditions. We organize cultural events, religious celebrations, community picnics, and educational programs that keep our heritage alive for future generations.

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Mission

To promote and preserve Hindu Sindhi culture, language, and values in the greater Seattle area by organizing cultural events, religious celebrations, and community programs that unite families and inspire future generations.

Our Vision

To be the premier Sindhi cultural organization in the Pacific Northwest — a vibrant, inclusive community where every Sindhi family feels connected to their roots and empowered to share their heritage with pride.

Our Values

Community, Culture, Compassion, and Connection. We believe in the power of togetherness, the beauty of our traditions, the importance of giving back, and the strength found in unity.

What We Do

Our programs and activities that strengthen the community

Cultural Celebrations

We organize major festivals like Cheti Chand, Diwali, Teejri, and Lal Loi with traditional rituals, cultural performances, and community feasts.

Language Programs

Sindhi language classes and storytelling sessions for children and adults to keep our mother tongue alive and thriving.

Community Service

Charitable initiatives, volunteering opportunities, and support programs that give back to both our community and the broader Seattle area.

Religious Events

Satsangs, bhajans, and prayer gatherings that nourish our spiritual well-being and connect us to our faith.

Youth Programs

Special activities for young Sindhis including cultural workshops, mentoring programs, and fun events that instill pride in their heritage.

Social Gatherings

Regular community meetups, potlucks, picnics, and get-togethers that strengthen bonds between Sindhi families in the area.
